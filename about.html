About me

Learn about the book writer

Traazel Rosario is an accomplished author whose work delves deep into the intricacies of love, identity, and societal expectations. Her latest novel, "Silver Lining," has captivated readers with its powerful narrative and heartfelt exploration of the human spirit. Traazel's storytelling prowess shines through in this compelling tale of two women who courageously navigate the oppressive confines of a conservative society to embrace their true selves and their love for each other.
